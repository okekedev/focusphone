Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll my child's iPhone?

Open the FocusPhone app on your iPhone or Mac, go to "Add Device", and scan the QR code with your child's iPhone camera. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the enrollment.

Can my child remove the management profile?

The management profile can be removed from Settings on the device. However, you'll receive a notification when this happens, and the device will no longer be managed by FocusPhone.

What apps can I restrict?

FocusPhone allows you to create profiles that control access to Phone, Messages, Contacts, Camera, and Photos. You can choose which apps are available based on your family's needs.

How does location tracking work?

When you tap "Find Device" in the app, we request the current location from the enrolled device. Location is only retrieved on-demand and is not continuously tracked.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission and storage. We never sell your data, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Do I need an Apple Developer account?

No. Parents can use FocusPhone with just their Apple ID. The Apple Developer account is only needed by the service administrator.

What iOS version is required?

FocusPhone requires iOS 17 or later on enrolled devices, and iOS 17 or macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later on the parent's device.

How do I cancel or delete my account?

You can delete your account from the Account tab in the FocusPhone app. This will remove all your data and unenroll all managed devices.
